How it works

Get out for a healthy walk and make a new friend in 3 easy steps

1. Schedule a walk

We will match you with a fellow walker from our wonderful community around the world.

2. Join your fellow walker

Hit the "Join" button on your dashaboard. Greet your partner on video. After brief intros, switch the call to audio mode. Start your walk and continue chatting!

3. Celebrate your Walk Together

Hybrid mode (default)

  • Introduce yourself on video, share where you are walking
  • Switch off video, continue chatting on audio only as you walk
  • At the end of the call, switch video back on.
  • Celebrate your walk and connection!
  • Thank your partner and sign off!


Audio only mode

The intros, the walk, the sign off, are all in audio only mode. Perfect for those situations where the data connection is spotty!
