The Science Behind Walk Together



Walking is one of the simplest yet most effective ways to improve your overall health and wellbeing.

Not only is it accessible to anyone, anywhere in the world, but it also offers a multitude of benefits that can transform your life.

With WalkTogether, you can experience these benefits while connecting with like-minded individuals from around the globe.



Easy, Simple, Costs $0

Walking requires no special equipment, training, or facilities.

You can walk anywhere, anytime, making it the perfect movement for busy lives.



Better Physical Health

Walking is an excellent way to maintain a healthy weight, reduce the risk of chronic diseases, and improve cardiovascular health.

Regular walking can also boost your immune system, reducing the risk of illness and infection. Plus, it's a low-impact exercise, making it perfect for people of all ages and fitness levels.



Happier, Better Emotional Health

Walking is a natural mood-booster, reducing stress and anxiety while improving overall mental wellbeing. It can even help alleviate symptoms of depression.

Walking releases endorphins, also known as "feel-good" hormones, which can leave you feeling happier and more energized.



Fitness Benefits

Regular walking improves your overall fitness, increasing energy levels and reducing fatigue.



Deep, Restful Sleep

Regular walking improves your sleep quality, across all sleep cycles, including deep sleep and REM cycle.

This plays a crucial role in better rest and recovery, focus and cognition, energy levels, and longevity.



Boost Performance at Work, Studying, and More

Walking can increase your creativity by up to 60%. It literally increases the volume of areas in your brain that influence memory and cognition capabilities.

When you walk regularly, you boost your performance at work, studying and any other cognitive activity, leading to greater productivity and success.



All the above benefits of Walking, get compounded when you walk with someone compared to walking alone.

Want to live longer, reduce loneliness and feel connected, increase joy and satisfaction from life?

... Walk with someone.



Improve Physical Health

Walking with a friend can increase motivation to exercise and maintain a consistent routine.

When you walk with someone, it improves cardiovascular health, including lower blood pressure and a reduced risk of heart disease.



Increase Happiness + Emotional Wellbeing

Walking with a friend can lead to improved mental well-being, including reduced stress and anxiety.



Stronger Social Connections + Social Support

Having a walking buddy can strengthen social connections and build stronger relationships.

It also provides emotional support and encouragement, leading to a greater sense of well-being.



Live Longer + Boost Life-Satisfaction

People with strong social connections, such as those formed through walking with friends, tend to live longer.

Research also shows that walking with friends leads to a boost in life-satisfaction, and overall happiness.
