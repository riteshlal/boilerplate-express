Why I made Walk Together

I'm an avid walker, and walking has been my lifeline. It has kept me physically fit and emotionally grounded, especially during some dark times.

After leaving Google and a rough divorce, I went on an year-long road trip across America. While the journey was incredible, I often felt the sting of loneliness. Sure, I'd schedule occasional calls with friends during my walks, but coordinating our busy lives was no fun.

That's when the idea for Walk Together hit me. I wanted an easy way to find a walking buddy, no matter where I was or what time it was. Walk Together was born out of that need - to combine the joy of walking and connect with others.

My goal was simple: walk anywhere, anytime, and let Walk Together find a friendly partner to join me. I hope you find it as helpful and life-changing as I do. Welcome! üôèüèΩ
